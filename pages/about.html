About Green Software Solutions OÜ

Green Software Solutions OÜ is an Estonia-registered software development company specializing in frontend UI/UX solutions that power modern business applications. By crafting intuitive and efficient user interfaces, the company ensures seamless interactions between users and complex systems.

Expertise includes designing and developing AI-powered dashboards, Microsoft Teams chatbots, and tailored sales tools with competitive analysis, messaging, goal tracking, and performance insights. Custom management panels and microfrontends further enhance business operations, integrating smoothly into existing infrastructures.

Beyond enterprise software, Green Software Solutions OÜ also creates sleek, user-friendly websites for artists, schools, and other organizations. A deep understanding of usability, aesthetics, and performance drives each project, delivering digital experiences that are both functional and engaging.
