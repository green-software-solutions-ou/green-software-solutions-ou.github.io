Free Software Projects

At Green Software Solutions, we believe in the power of open-source and free software to drive innovation and sustainability. Here are some of our free software projects that contribute to more environmentally friendly computing:

GreenMetrics

An open-source tool for measuring and optimizing the energy consumption of software applications. GreenMetrics provides developers with insights into their code's environmental impact and offers suggestions for more efficient alternatives.

EcoCache

A resource-efficient caching library designed to minimize memory usage and processing power while maintaining high performance. EcoCache intelligently manages data storage to reduce the environmental footprint of applications.

SustainableCI

A set of CI/CD tools and practices that optimize build processes to consume less energy. SustainableCI reduces the carbon footprint of development pipelines without sacrificing quality or speed.

All our free software projects are available on GitHub under open-source licenses. We welcome contributions from the community to help make software development more sustainable.
